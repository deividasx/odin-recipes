Rocket

It doesn't get more simple than that!

Perfect for any season and almost any dish.
This condiment goes especially well with our steak and potato recipes to create a classic triumverate ready to conquer your tastebuds!

Ingredients

  • Fresh Rocket
  • Parmesan Shavings
  • Olive Oil
  • Salt and Pepper

Steps

  1. Place all of the ingrediends in a bowl
  2. Toss to combine and coat

That is it! Very simple yet timeless.
All that is left to do now is to plate it up and enjoy!
