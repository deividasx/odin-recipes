Perfect Ribeye

A Ribeye Steak is one of the few joys of life.

Sweet as lullaby and the biggest plate of yams - it's one of the few true joys of life.
This recipe makes it simple to nail your cooking to a perfection and the infused butter will take it to the next level.

Ingredients

  • A Ribeye Steak
    • Taken out of the fridge 10 to 20 minutes before cooking to let it rest
    • Aim for more marbling when shopping as there is tons of flavour in the fat
    • Pat dry with a J-Cloth before cooking
  • A bulb of Garlic
  • Some fresh Thymre or Rosemary
  • 1/3 block of Butter
  • Salt and Pepper
    • You should aim to have both fine salt and some coarse salt, like Maldon
  • Splash of Oil

Steps

  1. Make the garlic-infused butter
    1. Heat the butter in a microwave to a room temperature
      (just soft enough that you can mix everything but not liquid)
    2. Peel and finely mince the garlic clove
    3. Chop or pluck the Rosemary or Thyme
    4. Ground some black pepper
    5. Combine everything until well mixed
    6. Set aside
  2. Heat the pan to a medium-high temperature
  3. Pat the streak dry if you havent done so using a disposable cloth
  4. Coat the steak with a tiny amount of oil and then a small amount of fine salt (more will be added at the end)
  5. Using a pair of tongs, hold the steak firmly and place it in the pan
  6. Keep turning the steak every 30-45 seconds until you reach you prefered doneness
    • 2-3 Minutes for Rare
    • 4-5 Minutes for Medium to Memdium Rare
    • 6-8 Minutes for Well-Done
      These are general guidelines for a steak that is 10 to 15cm thick
  7. On a plate, put a few spoons of the garlic butter and spread it to the size of your steak
  8. Take the steak out of the pan and place it on the plate, on top of the butter
  9. Put a few spoons of the garlic butter on top of the steak and let it rest for a few minutes before cutting into it
  10. Finish with Maldon salt to taste

Simple as that! Now enjoy it with your favorite sides and condiments before it gets cold!
