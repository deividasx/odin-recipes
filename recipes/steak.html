Perfect Ribeye

A Ribeye Steak is one of the few joys of life.

Sweet as lullaby and the biggest plate of yams - it's one of the few true joys of life.

This recipe makes it simple to nail your cooking to a perfection and the infused butter will take it to the next level.

Ingredients

  • A Ribeye Steak
    • Taken out of the fridge 10 to 20 minutes before cooking to let it rest.
    • Aim for more marbling when shopping as there is tons of flavour in the fat
    • Pat dry with a J-Cloth before cooking
